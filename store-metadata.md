## Description
RunPlan is your personal running coach with structured training plans for every level. Whether you're a complete beginner or training for a marathon, RunPlan has a plan for you.

**6 Training Plans:**
• Couch to 5K — Start from zero and build up to 5K in 8 weeks
• 5K Improve — Sharpen your 5K time with tempo and interval work
• 10K Beginner — Build from 5K to 10K in 8 weeks
• 10K Intermediate — Advanced 10K training with speedwork
• Half Marathon — 12-week plan with long run progression to 20km
• Marathon — Complete 16-week marathon preparation up to 35km

**Features:**
• Detailed week-by-week workout schedules
• Track completed workouts with notes
• Calendar view with color-coded workout types
• Progress stats, streaks, and motivational messages
• Dark mode support
• 100% offline — all data stored locally on your device

**Workout Types:**
Easy runs, tempo runs, intervals, long runs, cross-training, and rest days — all with proper periodization for safe, effective training.
